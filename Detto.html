    10. Detto FAQ(Created by MOS).

    1. What do you do in the game? A: What you basicly do in this game, so far is battle in singleplayer worlds and collect treasure dropped by the defeated Dettos or bosses. You can level up to different levels to gain higher stats. Moves found in defeated Detto's can last for either 2-4 uses before breaking. When you level up to a certain level you can evolve into a better Detto with a wider variety of moves that can be learned(Appearenes also will change.). More advanced Dettos can either be purchased, or found in special events that have a low percantage chance of dropping it's egg(hatched Detto's level can range from 1-8). Evo-Stones can be used to evolve your Detto into a different branch e.i. Water into Ice, or Air into Rust. Each branch has It's own types of abilities and moves that can be used for it, so choose wisely!
    2. Are there going to be any more updates? A:Yes, there is going to be constant updates and we will keep trying our best to keep this wonderful server running.
    3. What other features are you thinking of for big updates? A: I am thinking about adding a chat and multiplayer room, so players can battle each other and avoid being bored by just playing CPUs. I am also thinking of more evolutions, not just stopping at 2 or 3, but maybe 4 or 5, but we will see.
    4. Are there going to be any special events or items that I can get for a limited amount of time? A: Yes, there may be exclusive Detto's that will come with events and there may be constumes like Reindeer horns for special kinds of Detto's. There may be also some exclusive moves like during Thankgiving, Horn of Plenty that heals you or maybe Flock, and etc.
    5. Q: Can items and other things be bought with real money? A: Yes, but be sure you want that item or Detto, because there are no refunds.

    Herbs and effects

    1. Verr Herb: Awakens sleeping Detto's.
    2. Dreb Herb: Relieves a Detto from being tired
    3. Calm Herb: Relieves a Detto from shock
    4. Buff Herb: Increases attack for 2 turns

    Detto Moves and Effects

    1. Razor Claws: Raises attack by one stage and does damage.
    2. Evade: Raises chance of avoiding an attack.
    3. Flood: Does damage and has a 10% of drowning(KO) the enemy
    4. Hail: Starts hailing and does damage to non-water types.
    5. Shock: Becomes zapped with a small bolt of electricity.(Chance to become stiffened)
    6. Stomp: Causes confusion and does damage
    7. Steal: Raids your opponent of a herb.
    8. Shapeshift: Can be used by some detto's.
    9. Crush:Can only be used by heavy dettos. Does Massive Damage, but may cause dizziness.
    10. Static:Has a 25% of failing, but if it works, it does more damage to it's weakness/s
    11. Fog:Creates a fog that lowers everyones accuracy, stops in 3 turns.
    12. Lead Missile: Sharpened leaves that do damage over time and can hit up until it does 30% damage to the enemy.
    13. Sharpen:Raises the attack of the user by 1.
    14. Shards: Causes turn to turn damage on enemy.
    15. Store Energy:Raises Attack and Defense of usee
    16. ScapeGoat: Causes enemy to distract and attack that maximum can hold 30 HP.
    17. Cursed Fate:Loses HP over time and becomes confused.
    18. Mud Slide: Decreases enemies defense and does damage
    19. Barrier: Protects the user from damage, if continued use happens, it increases with a chance to fail.
    20. Hide: Become hidden into the surronding, moves that attack everywhere can hit hidden dettos.
    21. Poison Bindings: Poison your enemy and does damage(not counting the poison) every turn
    22. Combustion: Boiled heat inside one's body comes forth and does damage.
    23. Charm: Enemie's accuracy drops(Dettos with ability of Blind Spot).
    24. Heart Break: Breaks the enemies will to live(only love types can use this move.) Only counter is a soul(20 gold)
    25. Cyclone: Creates a funnel of air that wraps around enemies sometimes preventing moves.
    26. Consume: Has a 60% chance of eating a herb
    27. Analyze: Shows the opponents moveset
    28. Gravitational Reverse: Changes who attacks first, the heavier the detto, the faster it will be
    29. Mystic Power: An unknown power that can differ from 30-130 damage. Can be any typed move.
    30. Elemental Reverse: Changes your Detto's type for two turns. It reverses E.I. Fire to Water, Life to Death, etc.
    31. Blind Shot: Has 30% accuracy, but does about 50 damage.
    32. Flash Flood: Creates a sudden surge of water that sweeps enemy Detto's away.
    33. Tri-Punch: Hits the opponents with three rapid punches.
    34. Telekinesis: One of the moves that can unlock secret rooms, only in multiplayer.
    35. Illusion: Creates a illusion that decreases damage done.
    36. Torrent:Sweeps the enemy of their feet.
    37. Lava Blast: Shoots blobs of lava towards the enemy.
    38. Magic Punch: Throws a colorful, mystical punch of magic toward the enemy.
    39. Ocean's Curse: Turns the whole ocean against one.
    40. Hammer: Hammer's the enemy to dust
    41. Iceplosion: Creates a big explosion with ice flying everywhere.
    42. Dryout: Turns all moves to a earth move for 2 turns.
    43. Relax: Becomes tired and heals 30 hp every turn.
    44. Duplicate: Multiplies and becomes harder to hit.
    45. Trample: Tramples the enemy.
    46. Block:Raises Defense.

    Abilities and Their Effects.

    1. Blind Spot: Is immune to non-hurting attacks.
    2. Golden Core: Produces more gold, rubies and etc with every win
    3. Four Leaf Clover: Chance increases to get a Detto egg or item.
    4. Slippery: Has a higher chance to avoid physical attacks
    5. Sharpshooter: Has no chance of missing attacks.
    6. Fame and Glory: Earns more Exp if and Detto has this ability.

    Starting Character Type

    1. Control: Control Masters can merge some of their Detto's to create a Mega-Fusion(Otherwise if wanted a Mega-Fusion, you need a tranbiner).
    2. Attack: Attack Masters have 25% Chance of raising their Detto's Attack for two turns every time they defeat a Detto.
    3. Defense: Defense Masters have a 25% Chance of raising their Detto's Attack for two turns every time they defeat a Detto.
    4. Wisdom: Wisdom Masters have a 20% Chance of gaining extra exp after battles.

    Ranks

    1. Member: A lowly rank that can only use regular functions, such as the shop, breeding center and.
    2. Knight: A higher ranked person that has updates to extra new updates. You can earn this rank by completing Level 30.
    3. King: The highest ranked people, so far and have the treasure room open to them.(Treasure room is where you can go once a day to collect a very fine treasure.) They also have the chance to compete in the Ranked tornaments for prizes. Need to beat 45 Levels.
    4. Moderator: Helps around with stuff and gives the owner/admin ideas. These people can give warnings to inappropriate behavior, spam and etc. They can also ban people for doing the things just listed. Needs to be someone the admin trusts.
    5. Owners: There will some Owners around that help around with programming and fixing minor glitches. If you need help with a problem contact these guys. Can not be earned.
    6. Admin: The person that controls the whole game and has the nickname of MOS(Master of Shadows). They can do anything ranging from hacks, bans and rewriting this sentence.
